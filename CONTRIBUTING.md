# Contributing to Taiwan Diet Localization Guide

Thank you for your interest in contributing. This repository aims to be a reliable, professionally maintained resource for Taiwan-specific dietary guidance.

## Who Should Contribute

- **Registered Dietitians (RD)** with Taiwan clinical experience — corrections to nutritional data, practical guidance updates
- **Food scientists** with knowledge of Taiwan's food supply chain
- **Researchers** with access to Taiwan-specific nutritional studies or government datasets
- **Healthcare professionals** who identify outdated or inaccurate health claims

## What We Accept

- Corrections to nutritional data (with source citations)
- Additional food swap tables for underrepresented venues (hospital cafeterias, school lunch, etc.)
- Updated government guideline references
- New localization guides for additional dietary patterns (vegetarian, renal diet, etc.)

## What We Do NOT Accept

- Promotional content for specific brands or products
- Unsourced nutritional claims
- Content without a Taiwan-specific angle (plenty of general diet guides exist)

## How to Contribute

1. Open an issue first describing the change and its source
2. Fork the repository
3. Make your changes with clear commit messages
4. Add your source to the relevant `## Scientific Basis` section
5. Submit a pull request referencing the issue

## Citation Format

For academic papers:
> Author(s). (Year). "Title." *Journal* Volume(Issue):Pages. DOI.

For government sources:
> 機關名稱. (年份). 《文件名稱》. URL.

## Health Disclaimer

All contributions must maintain the existing health disclaimer. This guide is for educational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice. Contributors should not make diagnostic or treatment claims.
